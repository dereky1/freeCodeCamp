Shamu

The original killer whale Shamu

Timeline of Shamu's Life:

  • Unknown - Shamu was born in the wild
  • 1965 - Captured in October by Ted Griffin off Penn Cove, Puget Sound, Washington
  • 1965 - Shamu was sold to SeaWorld in San Diego in December
  • 1971 - Shamu retired from SeaWorld "Shamu" shows after attack trainer
  • 1971 - Shamu passed away in August

“What we should enjoy, perhaps, is not their performance, but the mere fact of their existence. That, we believe, is wonder enough.”

― David Kirby, Death at SeaWorld: Shamu and the Dark Side of Killer Whales in Captivity
